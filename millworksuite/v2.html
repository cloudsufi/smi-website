The Problem

Manual millwork
estimating is broken.

Every millwork estimator knows the drill: download a 300-page PDF, manually sort and rename every sheet, hunt through floor plans for elevation callouts, flip back and forth between pages, manually measure every casework item, re-enter it all into a pricing spreadsheet, format a proposal in Word, then re-type the winning items into Microvellum or CabinetVision from scratch.

It takes days. It introduces errors at every handoff. And it means your estimators are spending more time on data entry than on winning work.

Hours sorting and renaming drawing sheets — manually organizing drawing sets before any real work can begin.
Hunting elevation callouts across dozens of pages — no connection between floor plan markers and detail sheets.
Manual dimension transcription errors — scaling by hand introduces mistakes that compound through the entire estimate.
Re-entering scope into pricing spreadsheets — every item manually typed into Excel or a custom spreadsheet. One tool for takeoff, another for pricing — nothing connected.
Copy-pasting proposals in Word or InDesign — after pricing, the scope gets reformatted again for GC submission. Three tools, three manual handoffs, three chances to miss something.
Re-entering data into CAD — after winning, every cabinet item manually entered into Microvellum or CabinetVision from scratch.
Slow bid cycle — the longer it takes to estimate, the fewer bids you can submit and the less competitive your pricing becomes.
The Platform

Five modules. One platform.
Zero re-entry.

From PDF to priced proposal to CAD — without leaving the application, without re-entering data, and without patching together disconnected tools.

Module 01 · Intake
Auto Page Organization

Load any architectural PDF and every page is instantly renamed and organized by drawing set. No manual sorting, no renaming — regardless of how many sheets or revisions the set contains.

Module 02 · Scope
AI Elevation Detection

One click scans the entire sheet — room names, drawing scales, and millwork types identified in seconds. Countertops, vanities, base cabinets, upper cabinets — each tagged, classified, and ready for review.

Module 02 · Scope
Floor Plan Intelligence

The AI scans floor plans to detect rooms and elevation markers. Color-coded symbols count identical rooms for quantity estimates. Markers link directly to the page or elevation — every callout connected to its detail sheet.

Module 02 · Scope
Linked Detail & Split View

Click any room or elevation to open its detail alongside the floor plan and scope items — products, dimensions, notes and cross-references all in one synchronized view. Floor plans, elevations, and scope linked side by side in real time.

Module 02 · Scope
Calibrated Measurement Tool

Verify dimensions and calculate areas directly on the drawing. Totalize values across multiple pages. Distances computed automatically using the page scale — no manual conversion, no scale errors.

Module 03 · Pricing
User-Defined Pricing Engine

Input your own pricing data — material rates, labor rates, overhead — and apply them to the detected scope. Customize discounts per line item, per category, or globally. Your numbers, your rules. No generic cost databases.

Module 04 · Proposal
Proposal Generation

Your priced estimate generates directly into a professional scope of work proposal ready for GC submission. No re-entry, no copy-paste. Branded and formatted for client delivery — all from the same session.

Module 05 · Export
Excel & Direct CAD Export

Export to Excel with built-in pricing formulas for further review. Or push directly to Microvellum or CabinetVision — cabinet layouts auto-matched to your user-defined libraries. Shop drawings ready to start immediately.
