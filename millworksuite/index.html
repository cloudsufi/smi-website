The Broken Workflow
Industry Reality

Commercial millwork estimating hasn't changed in 40 years.

Three handoffs. Three places where a complex commercial bid goes from a fast quote to a multi-week scramble. We built MillworkSuite to collapse all three.

01 Drawings

Architectural PDFs arrive. An estimator manually marks up every cabinet, elevation by elevation, room by room. Major commercial bids consume entire estimating teams for weeks just to land on a count.

02 Pricing

That count gets copied into a spreadsheet. Pricing tables are applied manually. One formula error, one missed cabinet — and the bid is wrong before it leaves the door.

03 Handoff

The estimator sends the scope to the drafter. The drafter re-enters everything from scratch — cabinet codes, dimensions, configurations — into Microvellum or Cabinet Vision. The same job, entered twice.

MillworkSuite was built to take these tasks off their plate — not their jobs.
Our point of view
Our Point of View
Manifesto

The goal isn't to replace estimators or drafters. It's to let them work at their best.

Skilled people are scarce in this trade. The bottleneck isn't talent — it's the volume of repetitive work piled in front of it. MillworkSuite is built around a single principle: amplify expertise, eliminate repetition.

A Time Problem, Not a Skill Problem

Estimators don't bid slowly because they aren't good at it. They bid slowly because the work is mechanical.

Marking up cabinets in a PDF isn't expertise. Copying counts into a spreadsheet isn't expertise. Re-keying the same project into Microvellum isn't expertise. It's transcription dressed up as estimation.

The actual hard work — reading the room, anticipating field conditions, sequencing the install, optimizing the cost approach, catching what the architect didn't draw — is what your team is paid for. That's where their judgment shows up. That's where margin is won or lost.

MillworkSuite handles the volume so the people in your shop can spend their time on the work that requires them.

Revisions & Addendums
Revision-aware

Architects revise. Your scope shouldn't restart.

Drawings don't arrive once. Architects deliver a base set, then an addendum, then a post-award revision, then change orders. MillworkSuite handles every version — without rebuilding the job from scratch.

Diff revisions. Update only what changed. Push deltas to CAD.

Upload a new drawing set. MillworkSuite compares it page-by-page, elevation-by-elevation against the prior version. Added cabinets, removed cabinets, dimension changes, finish swaps — every difference is flagged and ready for review.

  • + Detect Page-level diffing. Match revised pages to the original set, even when pagination shifts.
  • ~ Reprice Re-price only the deltas. The unchanged scope holds. Your bid updates in minutes — not days.
  • → Push Export the revised job. Microvellum and Cabinet Vision get the updated project — drafter sees only what changed.
