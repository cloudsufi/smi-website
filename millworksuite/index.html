The Problem

Manual millwork
estimating is broken.

Every millwork estimator knows the drill: download a 300-page PDF, manually sort and rename every sheet, hunt through floor plans for elevation callouts, flip back and forth between pages, manually measure all the casework items — then do it all again when the drawings are revised.

It takes days-to-weeks. It introduces errors. And it delays your ability to submit competitive bids on time.

Hours sorting and renaming drawing sheets — manually organizing drawing sets before any work can begin.
Hunting elevation callouts across dozens of pages — no connection between floor plan markers and detail sheets.
Manual dimension transcription errors — scaling by hand introduces mistakes that compound errors throughout your estimate.
Re-entering scope into pricing spreadsheets — every item manually typed into Excel or a custom spreadsheet. One tool for takeoff, another for pricing — nothing connected.
Copy-pasting proposals in Word or InDesign — after pricing, the scope gets reformatted again for GC submission. Three tools, three manual handoffs, three chances to miss something.
Re-entering data into CAD— after estimating, every cabinet item manually entered into Microvellum or CabinetVision from scratch.
Slow bid cycle — the longer it takes to estimate, the fewer bids you can submit and the less time you can spend on closing the deal.
Core Features

Everything automated.
Nothing missed.

Six precision-engineered modules that eliminate manual work from drawing intake to final export — built exclusively for the millwork and casework industry.

Feature 01
Auto Page Organization

Load any architectural PDF and every page is instantly renamed and organized by drawing set. No manual sorting, no renaming, regardless of how many sheets or revisions the set contains.

Feature 02
AI Elevation Detection

One click scans the entire sheet — room names, drawing scales, and millwork types identified in seconds. Countertops, vanities, base cabinets, upper cabinets — each tagged, priced, and ready for review.

Feature 03
Floor Plan Intelligence

The AI scans floor plans to detect rooms and elevation markers. Color-coded symbols count identical rooms for quantity estimates. Page and Elevation markers link directly to the page or elevation — every callout connected to its detail sheet.

Feature 04
Linked Detail & Split View

Click any Room or Elevation to open its elevation alongside the floor plan and scope items — products, dimensions, notes and cross-references all in one view. Floor plans, elevations, and scope linked side by side in real time.

Feature 05
Calibrated Measurement Tool

Verify dimensions and calculate areas directly on the drawing. Totalize values across multiple pages. Distances computed automatically using the page scale — no manual conversion, no scale errors. Confirm every dimension before it enters your estimate.

Feature 06
User-Defined Pricing Engine

Input your own pricing data — material rates, labor rates, overhead — and apply them to the detected scope. Customize discounts per line item, per category, or globally. Your numbers, your rules. No generic cost databases.

Feature 07
Proposal Generation

Your priced estimate generates directly into a professional scope of work proposal ready for GC submission. No re-entry, no copy-paste. Branded and formatted for client delivery — all from the same session.

Feature 08
Scope Export to Excel & CAD

Export to Excel with your company's pricing rates ready for cost review. Or push directly to Microvellum or CabinetVision — cabinet layouts auto-matched to your user-defined libraries, jump start your shop drawings immediately.
