## SECTION 3: PROBLEM

### Headline
**Current:** Manual millwork estimating is broken.

**Revised:**
```
Manual millwork estimating is broken.
```
*(Keep as-is — this is strong.)*

### Body Copy
**Current:** Every millwork estimator knows the drill: download a 300-page PDF, manually sort and rename every sheet, hunt through floor plans for elevation callouts, flip back and forth between pages, manually measure all the casework items — then do it all again when the drawings are revised. It takes days-to-weeks. It introduces errors. And it delays your ability to submit competitive bids on time.

**Revised:**
```
Every millwork estimator knows the drill: download a 300-page PDF, manually sort
and rename every sheet, hunt through floor plans for elevation callouts, flip back
and forth between pages, manually measure every casework item, re-enter it all
into a pricing spreadsheet, format a proposal in Word, then re-type the winning
items into Microvellum or CabinetVision from scratch.

It takes days. It introduces errors at every handoff. And it means your
estimators are spending more time on data entry than on winning work.
```

**Why:** Extends the pain description to include pricing, proposal, and CAD re-entry — the full workflow pain that MillworkSuite solves. Current copy stops at the measurement stage.

### Pain Items
**Current pain items + new additions:**

```
1. Hours sorting and renaming drawing sheets
   — manually organizing drawing sets before any real work can begin.

2. Hunting elevation callouts across dozens of pages
   — no connection between floor plan markers and detail sheets.

3. Manual dimension transcription errors
   — scaling by hand introduces mistakes that compound through the entire estimate.

4. Re-entering scope into pricing spreadsheets                    [NEW]
   — every item manually typed into Excel, Alliance Millsoft, or a custom
   spreadsheet. One tool for takeoff, another for pricing — nothing connected.

5. Copy-pasting proposals in Word or InDesign                     [NEW]
   — after pricing, the scope gets reformatted again for GC submission.
   Three tools, three manual handoffs, three chances to miss something.

6. Re-entering data into CAD
   — after winning, every cabinet item manually entered into Microvellum
   or CabinetVision from scratch.

7. Slow bid cycle
   — the longer it takes to estimate, the fewer bids you can submit
   and the less competitive your pricing becomes.
```

**Why:** Pain items 4 and 5 are new and directly address the pricing and proposal gaps. The market analysis emphasizes that the "old reality" involves 5+ tools with manual handoffs between each.

### Section Header
**Current:** Everything automated. Nothing missed. / Six precision-engineered modules that eliminate manual work from drawing intake to final export.

**Revised:**
```
Five modules. One platform.
Zero re-entry.

From PDF to priced proposal to CAD — without leaving the application,

```

**Why:** "Zero re-entry" is the most powerful claim from the market analysis. The current header doesn't communicate the end-to-end value.

### Feature Cards (revised from 6 to 8 — adding Pricing Engine and Proposal Generation)

```
MODULE 01 · INTAKE
Auto Page Organization

Load any architectural PDF and every page is instantly renamed and organized
by drawing set. No manual sorting, no renaming — regardless of how many
sheets or revisions the set contains.
```

```
MODULE 02 · SCOPE
AI Elevation Detection

One click scans the entire sheet — room names, drawing scales, and millwork
types identified in seconds. Countertops, vanities, base cabinets, upper
cabinets — each tagged, classified, and ready for review.
```

```
MODULE 02 · SCOPE
Floor Plan Intelligence

The AI scans floor plans to detect rooms and elevation markers. Color-coded
symbols count identical rooms for quantity estimates. Markers link directly
to the page or elevation — every callout connected to its detail sheet.
```

```
MODULE 02 · SCOPE
Linked Detail & Split View

Click any room or elevation to open its detail alongside the floor plan
and scope items — products, dimensions, notes and cross-references all in
one synchronized view. Floor plans, elevations, and scope linked side by
side in real time.
```

```
MODULE 02 · SCOPE
Calibrated Measurement Tool

Verify dimensions and calculate areas directly on the drawing. Totalize
values across multiple pages. Distances computed automatically using the
page scale — no manual conversion, no scale errors.
```

```
MODULE 03 · PRICING                                               [NEW]
User-Defined Pricing Engine

Input your own pricing data — material rates, labor rates, overhead —
and apply them to the detected scope. Customize discounts per line item,
per category, or globally. The result is a firm-specific, fully editable
price sheet that reflects how your company actually prices millwork.
No generic cost databases. Your numbers, your rules.
```

```
MODULE 04 · PROPOSAL                                              [NEW]
Scope of Work Proposal Generation

Your priced estimate generates directly into a professional scope of work
proposal ready for GC submission. No re-entry, no copy-paste. The proposal
reflects the AI-detected scope, your pricing, and can be branded and
formatted for client delivery — all from the same session.
```

```
MODULE 05 · EXPORT
Excel & Direct CAD Export

Export to Excel with built-in pricing formulas for further review. Or push
directly to Microvellum or CabinetVision — cabinet layouts auto-matched to
your user-defined libraries. Shop drawings ready to start immediately
without re-entering a single item.
```

### Headline
**Current:** Upload drawings. / Export scope. / Start shop drawings.

**Revised:**
```
Upload drawings.
Price the scope.
Submit the proposal.
Start shop drawings.
```

**Why:** Current workflow headline skips pricing and proposal entirely.

### Workflow Steps (revised from 5 to 6)

```
Step 1: Upload Drawing Set
Load your PDF architectural drawings. Sheets are renamed and organized
by drawing set automatically.

Step 2: AI Scans & Detects
Elevations, rooms, scales, and millwork types — detected, classified,
and tagged in seconds across the entire drawing package.

Step 3: Review & Verify
Split view and calibrated measurement tool to cross-reference elevations,
floor plans, and dimensions. Confirm every item before it enters the estimate.

Step 4: Price the Scope                                           [NEW]
Apply your material rates, labor rates, and overhead. Customize discounts
per item or globally. Your numbers, automatically applied to every
detected scope item.

Step 5: Generate Proposal                                         [NEW]
One click produces a branded scope of work proposal — ready for GC
submission. No Word. No InDesign. No re-typing.

Step 6: Export to Excel or CAD
Excel with your pricing formulas, or direct push to Microvellum or
CabinetVision — libraries pre-matched, shop drawings ready to start.
```
